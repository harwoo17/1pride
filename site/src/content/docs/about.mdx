**1PRIDE** is two things in one:

- A **learning ladder** that takes you from zero to a deployable analytics app, built
  on real NFL data via [nflverse](https://github.com/nflverse/nflverse-data).
- A **portfolio piece** for the job search that proves SQL, Python, modeling, and
  full-stack data depth.

Built for one person first. Designed so other fans — Lions or otherwise — can run it.

## Why a football-org structure

Three reasons the structure earns its keep:

**Stakes you actually care about.** Lions football, Dan Campbell era. Domain
familiarity removes the friction of pretending to care about a fake dataset.

**A clean unlock loop.** Each level has a capstone and an unlock criterion. You move
up because you shipped the artifact, not because a week passed.

**A natural seniority arc.** Analyst pulls stats, Position Coach compares groups,
Head Coach makes situational calls, GM builds models, Owner ships the platform. The
org chart is the curriculum.

## A note on branding

1PRIDE uses Lions stats, public player names, public press-conference quotes,
and a Honolulu-Blue-ish palette because all of that is descriptive and not
trademarkable. The official Detroit Lions logo, the NFL shield, real
broadcast imagery, official player photography, and team-branded merchandise
designs do not appear here. 1PRIDE is not affiliated with or endorsed by the
Detroit Lions, the NFL, or any NFLPA-licensed entity.

The data layer is built so any team can be swapped in as the default.
