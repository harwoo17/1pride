Seven tiers. You start as an **Analyst** pulling stats on request and climb,
one shipped capstone at a time, to **Owner** — running the whole franchise,
end to end. Each rung earns its avatar; the ones above stay in silhouette
until you bank the rep.

## How you climb

Every tier has the same loop: **run the drills** in The Film Room, **ship the
capstone**, and the next rung unlocks — the silhouette resolves into a full
3D bust and a whistle blows. No tier passes by time; you advance because the
artifact is real.
