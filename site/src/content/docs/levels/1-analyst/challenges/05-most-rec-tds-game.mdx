<Fragment slot="prompt">
Find the Lions player-week in 2024 with the most receiving touchdowns. Return
the player, the week, the opponent, the receiving yards, and the receiving TDs.
Tie-break by receiving yards.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

A single row:

| player_display_name | week | opponent_team | receiving_yards | receiving_tds |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| *(player)* | *(int)* | *(team)* | *(yards)* | *(tds)* |

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

`ORDER BY receiving_tds DESC, receiving_yards DESC` for the tie-break, then
`LIMIT 1`. Watch the column name — it's `receiving_tds`, not `rec_tds`.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT player_display_name, week, opponent_team,
       receiving_yards, receiving_tds
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE recent_team = 'DET'
  AND season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
ORDER BY receiving_tds DESC, receiving_yards DESC
LIMIT 1;
```

Reasonable expectation: Amon-Ra or Jameson Williams, depending on the week.

</Fragment>
