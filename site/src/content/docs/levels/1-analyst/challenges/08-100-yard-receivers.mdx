<Challenge difficulty="starter">

<Fragment slot="prompt">
Return every Lions player-week in the 2024 regular season with **100 or more
receiving yards**. Sort by yards descending, tie-break by week.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

Many rows, four columns:

| player_display_name | week | receiving_yards | receiving_tds |
|---|---|---|---|
| *(player)* | *(int)* | *(int)* | *(int)* |

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

This is a `WHERE` + `ORDER BY` problem — no aggregates. Same skeleton as
Challenge 1 with the threshold bumped to 100 and no `LIMIT`.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT player_display_name, week, receiving_yards, receiving_tds
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE recent_team = 'DET'
  AND season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
  AND receiving_yards >= 100
ORDER BY receiving_yards DESC, week ASC;
```

Useful follow-up: change the threshold to 150 and see how many games survive.

</Fragment>
