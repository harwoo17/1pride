<Challenge difficulty="allpro">

<Fragment slot="prompt">
The Lions filter is gone. Return the **top 5 NFL rushers** of Week 1, 2024 —
their name, team, rushing yards, carries, and rushing TDs. Sort by rushing
yards descending, tie-break by TDs.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

Five rows:

| player_display_name | recent_team | rushing_yards | carries | rushing_tds |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| *(player)* | *(team)* | *(yards)* | *(carries)* | *(tds)* |

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

Drop the `recent_team = 'DET'` filter. Keep `season = 2024 AND week = 1`. Add
`carries IS NOT NULL` so you don't get a string of zero-carry receivers tied at
the bottom.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT player_display_name, recent_team, rushing_yards, carries, rushing_tds
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE season = 2024
  AND week = 1
  AND season_type = 'REG'
  AND carries IS NOT NULL
ORDER BY rushing_yards DESC, rushing_tds DESC
LIMIT 5;
```

Why this matters: every Level 2+ analysis needs *cross-team* queries. Getting
comfortable removing the team filter is its own small skill.

</Fragment>
