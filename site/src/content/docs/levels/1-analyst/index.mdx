**Identity.** You pull stats on request. You answer factual questions about what happened.

You are the entry-level voice in the room. Someone asks "who led the Lions in
rushing in Week 7?" and you have a query and a clean answer in under a minute.

## What you'll learn

| SQL | Python |
|---|---|
| `SELECT`, `WHERE`, `ORDER BY` | `pandas` basics, `read_csv` |
| `LIMIT`, `DISTINCT` | filtering with `.loc` and boolean masks |
| Basic aggregates (`SUM`, `AVG`, `COUNT`, `MAX`) | simple matplotlib plots |

## Capstone

**Lions 2024 season recap notebook.** Weekly leaders, defensive standouts,
week-over-week changes. One notebook, runnable end to end, with prose between cells
explaining what each query tells you.

## Unlock criteria

To advance to Level 2:

1. Clear **8 of 10** stat-lookup challenges below.
2. Ship the **season recap notebook** with at least one chart per category
   (offense, defense, special teams).
