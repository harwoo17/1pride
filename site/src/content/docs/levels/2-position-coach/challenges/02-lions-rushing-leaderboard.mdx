<Fragment slot="prompt">
Return Lions 2024 regular-season rushing **leaderboard**: player name, games
with at least one carry, total carries, total rushing yards, total rushing
TDs, yards per carry. Sort by yards descending.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

| player_display_name | games | carries | yards | tds | ypc |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| *(player)* | *(int)* | *(int)* | *(int)* | *(int)* | *(decimal)* |

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

`GROUP BY player_display_name`. `COUNT(*)` for games (already filtered to
rows with carries). YPC is `SUM(rushing_yards)::numeric / NULLIF(SUM(carries), 0)`.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT
    player_display_name,
    COUNT(*)                                                AS games,
    SUM(carries)                                            AS carries,
    SUM(rushing_yards)                                      AS yards,
    SUM(rushing_tds)                                        AS tds,
    ROUND(SUM(rushing_yards)::numeric / NULLIF(SUM(carries), 0), 2) AS ypc
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE recent_team = 'DET'
  AND season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
  AND carries IS NOT NULL
  AND carries > 0
GROUP BY player_display_name
ORDER BY yards DESC;
```

Montgomery and Gibbs on top, then situational backs, then Goff sneaks.
