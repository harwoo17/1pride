<Fragment slot="prompt">
Across the NFC North (DET, GB, MIN, CHI), return the top 15 **wide receivers**
by 2024 regular-season receiving yards. Show name, team, games, catches,
yards, TDs, yards per catch.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

15 rows, ordered by yards descending:

| player_display_name | recent_team | games | catches | yards | tds | yards_per_catch |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

`GROUP BY player_display_name, recent_team`. Filter `position_group = 'WR'`.
`recent_team IN ('DET', 'GB', 'MIN', 'CHI')`. `LIMIT 15`.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT
    player_display_name,
    recent_team,
    COUNT(*)                                                       AS games,
    SUM(receptions)                                                AS catches,
    SUM(receiving_yards)                                           AS yards,
    SUM(receiving_tds)                                             AS tds,
    ROUND(SUM(receiving_yards)::numeric / NULLIF(SUM(receptions), 0), 1) AS yards_per_catch
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE recent_team IN ('DET', 'GB', 'MIN', 'CHI')
  AND season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
  AND position_group = 'WR'
  AND targets IS NOT NULL
  AND targets > 0
GROUP BY player_display_name, recent_team
ORDER BY yards DESC
LIMIT 15;
```
