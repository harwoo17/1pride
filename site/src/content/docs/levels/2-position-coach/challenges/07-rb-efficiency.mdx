<Fragment slot="prompt">
NFL running backs in the 2024 regular season with at least **100 carries** and
**4.5+ yards per carry**. Return name, team, carries, yards, TDs, and YPC.
Sort by YPC descending.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

| player_display_name | recent_team | carries | yards | tds | ypc |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

Two `HAVING` conditions combined with `AND`. The YPC condition needs to repeat
the aggregate expression (no aliases inside `HAVING` in standard SQL).

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT
    player_display_name,
    recent_team,
    SUM(carries)                                       AS carries,
    SUM(rushing_yards)                                 AS yards,
    SUM(rushing_tds)                                   AS tds,
    ROUND(SUM(rushing_yards)::numeric / SUM(carries), 2) AS ypc
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
  AND position_group = 'RB'
  AND carries IS NOT NULL
GROUP BY player_display_name, recent_team
HAVING SUM(carries) >= 100
   AND SUM(rushing_yards)::numeric / NULLIF(SUM(carries), 0) >= 4.5
ORDER BY ypc DESC;
```

The expected starter for an All-Pro YPC season is 5.0+. The 100-carry filter
keeps QB scramblers out.

</Fragment>
