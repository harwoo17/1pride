<Challenge difficulty="starter">

<Fragment slot="prompt">
For Amon-Ra St. Brown in the 2024 regular season, return week, receiving
yards, previous week's receiving yards, and the week-over-week delta. Sort
by week. Week 1's previous-yards column should be NULL, not 0.
</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="output">

| week | yards | prev_yards | delta |
|---|---|---|---|

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="hint">

`LAG(receiving_yards) OVER (ORDER BY week)` for prev. Subtract for delta. No
`PARTITION BY` needed — you're already filtering to one player.

</Fragment>

<Fragment slot="solution">

```sql
SELECT
    week,
    receiving_yards AS yards,
    LAG(receiving_yards) OVER (ORDER BY week) AS prev_yards,
    receiving_yards - LAG(receiving_yards) OVER (ORDER BY week) AS delta
FROM weekly_stats
WHERE player_display_name = 'Amon-Ra St. Brown'
  AND season = 2024
  AND season_type = 'REG'
ORDER BY week;
```

Week 1 has `NULL` for both `prev_yards` and `delta` because there's no
previous week. That's correct — passing a default of `0` would make Week 1
look like a big positive delta, which is a lie.

</Fragment>
